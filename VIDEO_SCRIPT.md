# StudyMate AI — 5-Minute Demonstration Video Script

**Target Duration:** 4 minutes 50 seconds  
**Track:** Concierge Agents Track  

---

## ⏱️ Video Breakdown & Speaker Script

### 0:00 - 0:30 | The Problem (30s)
- **Visual:** Show split screen of scattered browser tabs—YouTube lectures, ChatGPT, Notion notes, Google Calendar, Quizlet.
- **Speaker:** "Hi everyone! Students and self-learners spend more time managing their learning than actually studying. We jump between ChatGPT for explanations, YouTube for lectures, Notion for schedules, and Quizlet for flashcards. This fragmentation leads to study burnout and static plans that never adapt when you struggle. Meet **StudyMate AI**—an autonomous AI learning companion built with Google ADK, C++, and Gemini."

### 0:30 - 1:00 | Why Agents & Architecture (30s)
- **Visual:** Display Architecture Diagram highlighting C++23 Backend, Python ADK service, SQLite, MCP tools, and Next.js frontend.
- **Speaker:** "Why agents? Traditional chatbots only give answers when prompted. StudyMate AI acts autonomously. Our C++ REST backend coordinates with a Python microservice running Google ADK. Six specialized subagents—Planner, Tutor, Research, Quiz, Progress, and Coordinator—work together with Model Context Protocol tools to plan, teach, test, and dynamically adapt your learning journey."

### 1:00 - 2:30 | Live Demo 1: Goal Planning & Navigable YouTube Transcripts (90s)
- **Visual:** 
  1. Open Next.js dashboard. Type goal: *"Master Machine Learning & Gradient Descent in 30 days"*. Click **Generate AI Study Plan**. Show instant roadmap creation.
  2. Navigate to **YouTube & Transcripts** tab. Show search for *"Gradient Descent"*. Video loads alongside interactive transcript.
  3. Click timestamp `04:00` in transcript -> Video player instantly jumps to `4:00`!
- **Speaker:** "Watch this. I type my goal: 'Master Machine Learning in 30 days'. The Planner Agent creates a daily schedule saved in SQLite. When I click on 'Gradient Descent', our Research Agent invokes MCP tools to fetch top YouTube lectures and timestamped transcripts. Look at this feature: clicking any transcript line instantly jumps the embedded YouTube video directly to that exact explanation!"

### 2:30 - 3:45 | Live Demo 2: AI Tutor, Adaptive Quizzes & Closed-Loop Replanning (75s)
- **Visual:**
  1. Click **AI Tutor** -> Show structured explanation with key takeaways.
  2. Click **Adaptive Quiz** -> Answer quiz questions. Intentional low score (40%).
  3. Show Adaptive Replanner notice: *"Mastery score 40%. Adding 1 revision day and YouTube deep-dive."* Show updated roadmap!
- **Speaker:** "Next, our Tutor Agent provides personalized concept breakdowns. Then I take an adaptive quiz generated by our Quiz Agent. If I struggle and score 40%, watch what happens: our Progress Agent detects the weak spot and triggers closed-loop replanning—automatically modifying tomorrow's schedule to add targeted revision!"

### 3:45 - 4:20 | Antigravity Visualizer & CLI Skill (35s)
- **Visual:**
  1. Open **Agent Traces** tab -> Show live Antigravity execution log stream.
  2. Switch to terminal -> Run `studymate explain --topic "Gradient Descent"`.
- **Speaker:** "For course evaluation criteria, our dashboard features a live Antigravity Agent Visualizer showing real-time subagent execution traces. We also built the `studymate` CLI tool, allowing users to run agent skills like `studymate explain` directly from the terminal!"

### 4:20 - 4:50 | Conclusion & Impact (30s)
- **Visual:** Show full stack running in Docker (`docker-compose up`). Show GitHub repo link.
- **Speaker:** "With a high-performance C++ backend, Google ADK multi-agent orchestration, MCP integration, and one-command Docker deployment, StudyMate AI turns study stress into autonomous success. Thank you!"
